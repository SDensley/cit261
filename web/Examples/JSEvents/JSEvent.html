JavaScript Events Example

This page displays the contents of rdi.json in a nice user friendly format. When you click on an image the top section will display the details of that character. As the display is changing notifications will display indicating states of animation change. As you mouse over the characters, the transition counter will increase. Upon touching this paragraph ad moving your finger, the location of your finger will be displayed below the transition count. Upon touching the details it will spin around.

In previous assignments, I have used click and mouse events.
